Guidelines for entering Correct text

----------------------------------------------

•Subject, Middle and Predicate

1. Subject is represented as "S"
2. Middle is represented as "M"
3. Predicate is represented as "P"

• Types of statements possible:

1. All S are M
2. No S are M
3. Some S are M
4. Some S are not M

• For Negation use the Symbol"~" without any whitespace as in example below:

Statement 1: All S are ~M
Statement 2: All ~M are P
Conclusion: All S are P

• First Statement:

1. You can switch between Subject(S) and Middle(M)
2. Example1: All S are M
3. Example2: All M are S

• Second Statement:

1. You can switch between Middle(M) and Predicate(P)
2. Example1: Some M are not P
3. Example2: Some P are M

• Conclusion:

1. You CANNOT switch between Subject(S) and Middle(M)
2. Example1: All S are P
3. Example2: Some S are P
